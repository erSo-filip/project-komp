History

Beaky Dargus is a small Irish brewery in West Wicklow created out of a passion for craft beer.

We have always enjoyed tasting different craft beers from around the world and especially here in Ireland. After many years of consuming a wide range of beers we thought we could have a go at doing it ourselves…

And so…

In 2015 Beaky Dargus was born!


We brewed our first batch for the market in 2016 The ’Black Beak Juggler’ Stout and since then have added a few more! ’Golden Melody’ Strong Ale, ’Solo Pogo Hop’ Single IPA and of course the ever popular ’Tea Lady’ Earl Grey Tea Ale.

We hope you enjoy our Beers!

Dariusz, Angus and Ger.
